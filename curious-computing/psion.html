### ![ARM](http://www.arm.com/images/site/arm_logo.gif)710T With MMU, Clocked at 18Mhz (32Mhz on the 5MX). ### Stories and Significance EG: One of very few ARM devices to have an 'ARM Powered' logo on the bottom of it. This thing would run for two weeks with just AA batteries, and had a 640px wide display. The keyboard is really, really good - I could type at 60 wpm when I was used to it. ### Software EG: EPOC32, which became Symbian OS. The OS came bundled with a Word, Excel and Access equivalent, as well as a BASIC (OPL, actully) interpreter. It is also possible to install Linux on the Psion 5, but you realise how well designed EPOC32 is when you do that... ### Owner: Jonathan Austin ### Picutres ![Psion Logo](http://www.spencer.nildram.co.uk/images/psilogo.gif)
